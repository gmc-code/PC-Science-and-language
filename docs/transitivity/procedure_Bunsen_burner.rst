========================================
Lighting the Bunsen burner
========================================


Procedure
--------------------

#. Place the Bunsen burner on the heatproof mat.
#. Connect the rubber tubing to the gas tap.
#. Close the collar.
#. Light the match.
#. Hold the lit match above the barrel of the Bunsen burner.
#. Turn on the gas.
#. Shake out the match.
#. Observe the flame.
#. Turn off the gas.
