====================================================
PC-Science-and-language introduction
====================================================

PC-Science-and-language is a resource for teachers and students to explore the relationship between science and language.
The aim is to help students develop their scientific literacy and communication skills by understanding the linguistic choices that make scientific writing effective.
The resources include examples of how to apply these linguistic choices in writing about various types of science writing.
By using this resource, students can improve their ability to communicate scientific ideas clearly and effectively.
